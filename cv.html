SUMMARY

More than 15 years of IT experience in multiple fields, Mario started at EDS as a Helpdesk agent and excelled in the junior position for 1 year. Then he was promoted to the role of Help Desk Team Leader serving for 4 years. Subsequently, Mario assisted for 3 years the Service Delivery Manager with the supervision of 22 employees. He developed a desktop application in .Net to promote efficiency in service levels with automatic calculation of SLAs times and facilitating the tracking of staff shift schedules.

In terms of education, Mario completed 21 credits of the Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at Université du Québec en Outaouais and expects to obtain his diploma in 2018. He completed the program Computer Programmer Analyst at La Cité collegiale in Ottawa and graduated with excellent grades. Simulataneously, he worked as a tutor assisting peers in their learning of programming techniques.
